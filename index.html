What is the Difference Between Logitech Meetup and Rally?

Logitech Meetup and Rally are both video conferencing cameras, but they have some differences.


Meetup is primarily designed for small meetings, small conference room sizes, small conference rooms only, and huddle spaces, while Rally is better suited for larger conference rooms.


Rally also has additional features such as the ability to pan, zoom, tilt, and zoom with greater precision and a separate speaker and microphone with an active speaker system for improved audio quality.


However, Meetup has smaller rooms, a wider field of view, and is more affordable than Rally. It also has built-in audio capabilities, making it a great all-in-one solution for smaller meeting spaces and large conference rooms.


Ultimately, the choice between Meetup and Rally depends on the size of your meeting room and your specific needs. If you have a small space and don't require advanced environmental control or audio features, Meetup may be the better choice. But if you need more precise lighting conditions, camera control, and high-quality audio for a larger space, Rally is the way to go.
